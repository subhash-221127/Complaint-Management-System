About CityFIx

CityFIx is a comprehensive government complaint management system designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government agencies. Our platform provides a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly way to report civic issues and track their resolution.

Our Mission

To empower citizens by providing a digital platform that ensures accountability, transparency, and efficient resolution of civic complaints, ultimately improving the quality of life in our communities.

Our Vision

A future where every citizen has a voice in governance, where complaints are addressed promptly, and where government services continuously improve through citizen feedback and data-driven insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a complaint?

Click on "Register" to create an account, then "Login" and use the complaint filing form to submit your issue with details and supporting documents.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Resolution time varies by complaint type and department workload. You'll receive regular status updates and estimated completion timelines.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures to protect all personal information and complaint details.

Can I track my complaint status?

Absolutely! Once logged in, you can view all your complaints and their current status in your dashboard.

What types of complaints can I file?

You can file complaints about public services, infrastructure issues, environmental concerns, safety hazards, and other civic matters.

How do I contact support?

Use the contact form below or call our 24/7 support hotline. Government officers can also reach out through the admin portal.
